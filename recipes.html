No Bake Strawberry Cheesecake

You will need a pre made graham cracker pie crust, strawberry yogurt, and whipped cream.
  1. First, you will mix the yogurt and whipped cream in a large bowl.
  2. Next, put the mixture in the graham cracker crust.
  3. Put the all of it in the feezer for 30 minutes.
  4. Serve and Enjoy!
